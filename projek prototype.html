Thanos

Villain in Marvel Comics

Thanos is a supervillain appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics.

Created by writer-artist Jim Starlin, the character first appeared in The Invincible Iron Man #55.

An Eternal Deviant warlord from the moon Titan, Thanos is regarded as one of the most powerful beings in the Marvel Universe

How The Writes Came Up With Thanos

Jim Starlin conceived Thanos during a college psychology course and introduced him as a villain in The Invincible Iron Man #55 (February 1973).

Thanos was based on the Freudian concept of Thanatos, more commonly referred to as the death drive.
